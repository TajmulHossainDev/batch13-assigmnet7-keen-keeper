#Project-KinKeeper:
kinkeeper is a friends relationship management app that helps you to connect with your friends and relative.

#Technology use:
--React JS
--React Router
--Tailwind css
--DaisyUI
--Recharts
--React Toostify

#Feature:
--view all your friends and realtives id for connect.
--log calls, texts, video for interactions in one click.
--visualize your interactions history with pie charts
